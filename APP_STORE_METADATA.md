**Description:**

Are you tired of losing track of your favorite videos, reels, and stories? Meet ClipVault, your personal offline media manager designed to keep your most important content accessible anywhere, anytime.

ClipVault provides a clean, distraction-free environment to archive your own content or media you have permission to save. Forget cluttered camera rolls and buffering screens—ClipVault downloads in high quality and organizes your videos, photos, and reels into a beautiful grid library for effortless offline playback.

Features:
- **Instant Save:** Paste a link and download your media directly to your secure vault.
- **Offline Access:** Enjoy your media fully offline with our native high-performance player.
- **Smart Filters:** Instantly filter your library by Videos, Photos, Reels, and Stories.
- **Camera Roll Sync:** Seamlessly export videos and photos to your iOS Photos app.
- **Privacy First:** No accounts required, no tracking, and no data collection. Everything stays strictly on your device.

Download ClipVault today and take permanent control of your digital content. (Intended for personal and authorized content only).
